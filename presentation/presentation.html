Mathematical Rigor / Slide 04

Resolving the
mAP Paradox.

Lightweight models suffer severe precision drop-offs in early epochs. Evaluating tiny architectures under legacy coarse metrics creates highly misleading performance gaps.

Legacy 11-point Inflation

Coarse $\max$ operations backward-propagate sparse spikes across broad recall bins, artificially inflating metrics by ~21.6% in lightweight models.
